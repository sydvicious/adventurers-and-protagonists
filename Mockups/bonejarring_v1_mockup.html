Scope reminder. These are v1-accurate flow/layout sketches for the basic-combatant sheet only — not the eventual full app. Deliberately absent (deferred): skills, spells, conditions, equipment/encumbrance, avatar, combat mode/HUD, sync, accounts. Styling intent: stock Apple (grouped lists, system colors, Dynamic Type, light/dark) — these mockups approximate that, the real SwiftUI app should look native. Accessibility north star: no meaning by color alone, calm/low-clutter, generous legibility. See vision.md §5a and §5c.
